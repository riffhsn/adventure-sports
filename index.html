About Us

Our activities are designed not only to let you have an amazing, fun and thrilling experience, but also burn calories, reduce stress, pump adrenaline, improve fitness and get a healthy dose of Vitamin D. Adventure Sports began as a lifestyle watersports brand in 1991, operating with resorts and leisure destinations at Turkey’s Aegean and Mediterranean coastal resorts. In 1998, Adventure Sports embarked on its first global expansion to the UAE. Today, we are recognized as the partner of choice for lifestyle venues, adventure seekers and 5* star resorts.
